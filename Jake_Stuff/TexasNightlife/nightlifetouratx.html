NightLife Group Tours Austin

Coming to Austin for your bachelor or bachelorette party? Maybe having a work networking event? Let Texas Nightlife help you! We offer an array of packages, or you can fully customize your experience. All you have to do is book the package, tell us how many people, answer a few questions, and we mail you your wristbands, itinerary, and get you where you need to go all for one flat rate.

All packages include a photographer, Red Bull, an Urban Pilot and a ride home at the end of the night to one location.

Select a package

Texas Package:

Want the Texas Experience? We will set you up at the bars that wearing a great pair of cowboy boots will fit right in. These bars don’t have bottle service, and don’t require a dress code. The country music will be blaring and dancing is encouraged.

Your beverage selection will consist only of Texas Made brand, and your party will have a spend at each bar for the time you are there.

The Royalty Package:

Only the finest for you and your party. We take you around the town in a limo, stopping at all the hot spots. You’ll have a table set up waiting for you with Red Bull and 2 top shelf bottles. No lines, no covers, just VIP treatment for the night.

Make sure you dress to impress for this one, as there are dress codes for this package.

The Dirty Package:

This one is for world famous Dirty 6 only in Austin Texas! You will bar hop the hot spots on Dirty 6 all night with a tab at each bar for you and your group. The bars are loud, wild and there is a lot of live music.

Let lose, sing along, and see where the night takes you. Your group will be able to walk from bar to bar with ease.

Up In The Club

DJs, Bass, and Nightlife. We will take your group to the hottest nightclubs around town. The DJs are spinning, bottle service is flowing, and you can get your dance on.

Make sure to dress appropriately for these clubs, most have a dress code and you’ll wanna look hot for this tour.

Locals Package:

Wanna hit all the local spots? These are the bars that don’t make those “paid to play” sites. This is where locals in Austin knows where to go for that hometown feeling.

This package takes your group all over town to see places not just on 6th street Austin. Most have that dive bar feel, while always having the best vibe. If you are from out of tome, this is the tour for you when in town for a few days.

Go West Package:

Not into dive bars? This package offers you all West 6th Street Bars for the night. A little more upscale, but no need to dress to nice. Afterall, Austin is more laidback. You’ll hit bars that offer all kinds of fun, from old school video games, to dancing to rooftop bars.

You and your group will be able to walk from bar to bar with your Urban Pilot for the night.

Custom Package:

Want to see an array of bars in Austin? Let us know what you prefer and we will make sure you get a good mix of experiences. This package will be built just for you and your party. Price will fluctuate according to what your preferences are. Please contact us via email to set up.

*Each member of your party will need to show ID at the door of each venue. If any party member is denied entry for any reason such as underage, overly intoxicated, or not dressed to code, no refunds will be given. Please dress appropriately.
